AMETHYST

Dec 23, 2023

New York City

Amethyst, a stunning purple quartz, is renowned for its beauty and metaphysical properties, believed to promote clarity of mind and inner peace. It's a popular choice in jewelry and spiritual practices, cherished for its calming energy and striking hue.

BORNITE

Mar 19, 2023

New York City

Bornite, also known as peacock ore, exhibits a mesmerizing iridescent surface with vibrant hues ranging from blue to purple. This copper sulfide mineral is prized by collectors for its striking appearance and is often used in jewelry and decorative pieces.

GALENA

May 15, 2022

St. Louis, MO

Galena, a lead sulfide mineral, is recognized for its metallic luster and distinctive cubic crystal structure. Historically valued for its lead content, Galena is also appreciated in crystal healing practices for its grounding properties and connection to the earth element.

KYANITE

June 11, 2022

New York City

Kyanite, a blue silicate mineral, is renowned for its remarkable anisotropic properties, displaying different colors when viewed from different angles. Resonating with the throat chakra, Kyanite is believed to promote communication, self-expression, and alignment of one's energy centers.

LABRADORITE

Labradorite, known for its stunning iridescence called labradorescence, captivates with flashes of blue, green, and gold hues. This feldspar mineral is cherished for its mystical properties, believed to enhance intuition, protect against negative energies, and promote spiritual awakening.
